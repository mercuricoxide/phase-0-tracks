Here is some info on kittens

Kittens are born to a litter and, without exception, are adorable and fuzzy. They also:

  • sleep all day
  • eat when not sleeping
  • love to play

Kittens then grow up to be cats

Kittens become cats after 6 months. They are still cute like kittens, but may display:

  1. irritability
  2. anti-socialism
  3. aloofness
